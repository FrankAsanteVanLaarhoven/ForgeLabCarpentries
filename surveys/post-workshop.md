# Post-Workshop Survey (question bank)

Send **immediately** at the end. Mirror the pre-workshop confidence questions so you can measure
change. Keep it under 5 minutes. This is the "Reaction" + "Learning" layer of our
[evaluation model](../community/retrospective-template.md).

## Reaction (NPS + satisfaction)

1. How likely are you to recommend this workshop to a colleague? (0–10) — **NPS**
2. Overall, the workshop was — Excellent / Good / OK / Poor
3. The pace was — Too slow / About right / Too fast
4. The balance of demo vs hands-on was — Too much talking / Just right / Too much exercise

## Learning (paired with pre-survey)

Rate your confidence **now** (1 = none, 5 = very confident):

5. Using the command line / shell. — 1–5
6. Using Git and GitHub. — 1–5
7. Writing a small script to automate a task. — 1–5
8. Working with ROS 2 / robot software. — 1–5  *(robotics workshops)*
9. Connecting AI models to perception/planning. — 1–5  *(AI workshops)*

10. Name one thing you can now do that you couldn't before today.

## What worked / what to improve

11. What was the **most** valuable part?
12. What was the **least** valuable, or most confusing?
13. How were the instructors and helpers? Did you feel included and supported?
14. Did anything in the setup or environment get in your way?

## Intent (bridge to Behavior)

15. Will you apply something from this workshop in the next month? — Yes / Maybe / No
16. If yes, to what?
17. Are you interested in the next track, a capstone, or becoming a helper/instructor?

## Open

18. Any other feedback for us?
