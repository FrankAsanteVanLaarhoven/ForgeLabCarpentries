# Instructors

The instructor pipeline is the single biggest differentiator of the Carpentries model — and of
ForgeLab. Great workshops come from well-trained instructors, not just good materials.

## The path

```
Attend a workshop → Helper → Instructor Training → Checkout (certify) → Instructor → Trainer
```

You don't need to be an expert in every topic to teach — you need to be a good facilitator who
keeps learners coding, included, and unstuck. The training focuses on exactly that.

## Quick principles

- **Never teach alone.** Co-teach with clear roles.
- **Teach the loop:** short demo → learner practice → debrief.
- **Inclusion is a skill**, and it's trainable. We train it explicitly.
- **Feedback is the product** — collect it every session and act on it.
