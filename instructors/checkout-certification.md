# Checkout & Certification

"Checkout" is how a trainee becomes a certified ForgeLab instructor. It confirms both pedagogy
and the ability to facilitate a real session — not subject-matter trivia.

## Requirements

To certify, a trainee must:

1. **Complete instructor training** (all modules — see [training-curriculum.md](training-curriculum.md)).
2. **Deliver a teaching demo** of a ForgeLab episode segment, observed or recorded, that
   demonstrates:
   - Clear learning objective stated up front.
   - Live coding with narration (typed, not pasted).
   - At least one formative-feedback check.
   - Inclusive facilitation (pacing, language, attention).
   - A clean recovery from at least one question or mistake.
3. **Co-teach or teach a real segment** of an actual workshop (observed by a certified
   instructor or trainer).
4. **Read and agree** to the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Evaluation rubric

| Criterion | Looking for |
|-----------|-------------|
| Objectives & structure | states what learners will *do*; follows demo→practice→debrief |
| Live coding | types and narrates; legible; manageable pace |
| Formative assessment | checks understanding; adjusts to signals |
| Inclusion | welcoming language, equitable attention, psychological safety |
| Recovery | handles a question/error calmly and instructively |

Feedback is specific, kind, and actionable. A trainee who isn't ready gets concrete next steps
and another attempt — checkout is supportive, not a gate to fear.

## Recording certification

When a trainee passes, record it in the [dashboard](dashboard.md):
name, date certified, certifying instructor, and the demo link/notes.

## Staying active

- Co-teach or teach at least one workshop per year.
- Participate in the community of practice (observation swaps, retrospectives).
- Optional growth: **Lesson Development Training** and **Train-the-Trainer**.
