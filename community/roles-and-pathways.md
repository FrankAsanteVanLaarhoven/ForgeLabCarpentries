# Community Roles & Pathways

ForgeLab grows by helping people move along a clear, welcoming progression. No stage is a
gate — everyone is valued where they are, and every step up is supported.

```
Learner → Helper → Instructor → Maintainer / Lesson Developer → Trainer → Organizer
```

## The roles

### Learner
Attends workshops. The reason we exist. Encouraged to ask questions, make mistakes, and — when
ready — come back as a helper.
**Move up by:** attending a workshop and volunteering to help at the next one.

### Helper
Supports learners during a workshop — circulating in person or monitoring chat/breakouts
online, unblocking setup and exercises. The best on-ramp to teaching.
**Move up by:** helping at 1+ workshops, then enrolling in [instructor training](../instructors/training-curriculum.md).

### Instructor
Certified to teach ForgeLab workshops. Facilitates the demo→practice→debrief loop, co-teaching
with clear roles. Subject mastery is *not* required for every topic — facilitation is.
**Move up by:** completing training + [checkout](../instructors/checkout-certification.md).

### Maintainer / Lesson Developer
Owns one or more lessons or tracks: reviews PRs, keeps content correct and current, authors new
episodes. Takes the optional **Lesson Development Training**.
**Move up by:** consistently contributing to a lesson and being invited to maintain it.

### Trainer
Runs instructor training and certifies new instructors. Scales the community.
**Move up by:** the **Train-the-Trainer** pathway after experience as an instructor.

### Organizer
Coordinates workshops, hosts, schedules, and partnerships; part of the lightweight core team.
See [GOVERNANCE](../GOVERNANCE.md).

## How we support movement

- Pair newcomers with experienced people (helper ↔ instructor, trainee ↔ trainer).
- A community of practice: regular meetings, observation swaps, shared retrospectives.
- Recognition for volunteers — visible credit, references, and badges.

## Recognition

Contributions of every kind count: teaching, helping, authoring, reviewing, hosting,
organizing. We record them (with consent) in the [instructor dashboard](../instructors/dashboard.md)
and thank contributors publicly.
