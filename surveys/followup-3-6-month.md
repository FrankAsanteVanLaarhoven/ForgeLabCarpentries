# 3–6 Month Follow-Up Survey (question bank)

Send 3–6 months after the workshop. This is the **Behavior / Results** layer — the hardest and
most valuable to measure. It tells us whether skills actually transferred into practice, and it
produces the success stories that build credibility and attract partners.

Keep it short and respectful of time. Offer to keep responses anonymous in any public reporting.

## Use & retention

1. Have you used any skills from the workshop since attending? — Often / Sometimes / Rarely / Not yet
2. Which skills have you used most? (shell / Git / scripting / CI-CD / ROS 2 / AI / cloud / blockchain)
3. Has anything from the workshop changed how you work? How?

## Impact

4. Has it saved you time, reduced errors, or enabled something new? Please describe.
5. Have you taught or shared any of it with others? — Yes / No (if yes, roughly how many people?)
6. Did it contribute to a project, paper, product, or grant? (optional details)

## Success story (optional, with permission)

7. Would you share a short story of how you applied what you learned?
   We may feature it (with your permission) in an impact report.
8. May we contact you about it? — Yes / No

## Continuing engagement

9. Have you taken another ForgeLab track or started a capstone? — Yes / Interested / No
10. Are you interested in becoming a helper or instructor?
11. What would help you go further? (more advanced topics, mentoring, community, etc.)

## Open

12. Anything else you'd like to share with us?
